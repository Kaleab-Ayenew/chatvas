Features

Everything you need for
conversation exploration

A purpose-built desktop app that transforms how you interact with AI conversations.

Infinite Canvas

Pan, zoom, and navigate freely across an unlimited workspace. Your conversations have no boundaries.

Auto Branching

When ChatGPT opens a new chat branch, it automatically becomes a new node on your canvas — connected visually.

Real ChatGPT Inside

Each node embeds a full ChatGPT session. Use your own account, with all features — plugins, GPTs, everything.

Visual Connections

Animated edges show the relationship between parent and branched conversations. See your thought process at a glance.

5 Beautiful Themes

Switch between Midnight, Nord, Rosé Pine, Solarized Dark, and Light. One click to change the vibe.

Cross Platform

Runs natively on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Same great experience everywhere.

How It Works

Up and running in seconds

No setup, no API keys, no configuration. Just download, open, and start branching.

1

Open the App

Launch Chat Nodes Canvas. A ChatGPT node is ready for you on the canvas.

2

Chat & Branch

Use ChatGPT normally. When you branch a conversation, a new node appears automatically.

3

Explore & Compare

Zoom out to see your full conversation tree. Compare branches side-by-side.
